Welcome to the Supporting Life wiki!

The Supporting LIFE project is designed to provide low-cost, effective, and targeted interventions in remote and resource-poor settings in order to overcome inadequate healthcare infrastructures in Malawi, Southern Africa.

The Supporting LIFE project aims to support the healthcare workers at the patient point of care. It utilises established technologies to circumvent the absent or limited healthcare infrastructure by exploiting the cellular telecommunications network, vital sign sensor technologies and point of care decision support systems.

If you are new to the project, then you may wish to begin at the 'About Supporting Life' page.
